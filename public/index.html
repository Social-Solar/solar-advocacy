About

The I LIKE SOLAR app gives people with solar the ability to share they have it using Facebook. It also helps people who are interested in getting solar learn which of their friends has it.

Great ideas are contagious and solar is a perfect example. Research shows that when someone knows someone with solar, their decision to get solar is made three times faster! (2013 Environ. Res. Lett. 8 014044). And that’s because when your friends recommend something -- a restaurant or a new band -- you know them and trust what they suggest. So let’s share the power of solar with friends.

Created by the Solar Energy Industries Association, The Climate Group and major solar companies, I LIKE SOLAR

is a solar community and a platform for sharing stories, ideas and the benefits of the sun. We believe solar represents a massive opportunity to transition to clean, renewable energy. Solar is an economic engine, fueling jobs and small businesses across America. Driven by a 40% price drop over the past two years, there is currently enough solar capacity to power more than 1.7 million American homes , with much more to come, making solar more affordable – and more popular – than ever.
